CTRC helps adults make the right choices about their support.

  • CTRC Supports each Service User as an individual, listening to and working with each person
  • CTRC uses a person centred approach to help each Service User plan his/her support and make the most of his/her allocated budget
  • CTRC Helps Service Users decide who will give support to help him/her achieve goals and aspirations
  • CTRC Support Workers adapt to Service Users’ needs and aspirations as they change, helping them access social, leisure, education and employment opportunities and activities within their local community

The support CTRC provides is tailored to meet each person’s individual needs and requirements, enabling them develop and maintain their independence, build and maintain their circle of support, develop and enhance their life skills and live life to the full.

Our person centred approach is CTRC’s way of making sure that we do a good job of listening to what you really want

CTRC will listen to you and help you think about what you want now and in the future.

CTRC will work with you, your family and friends, Social Service and Health Service professionals to enable you reach your full potential. We also work with charities and other community projects to assist you in work experience and gain confidence within the community.

CTRC Support Workers help you to make the changes you want in your life putting you at the centre of planning for your life.
