CTRC Community Interest Company has worked with a number of organisations that have helped us have a positive impact on the disabled community. Some of the organisations include:

  • Dementia Friends
  • MIND (mental health charity)
  • DASH (Disablement Association Hillingdon)
  • HAS (Hillingdon Association of voluntary services)
  • Hillingdon CCG (NHS)
  • Autistic Society

Our past partnership with private company Training Partners LTD resulted in the setup of a holiday scheme, for disabled people with a health benefit that provides disabled people an opportunity to travel throughout the UK and abroad. For more information please see Supported Holidays.

As we are always seeking to increase our impact on the community we are interested in working with people and organisations, in both the private and voluntary sector, that share a similar passion and enthusiasm for helping disabled people in the community.

Our management team are interested in hearing new ideas on ways to help people in the disabled community, so if you have any or would like to discuss them you may contact us.
