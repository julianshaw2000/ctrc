CTRC (community training and Respite care) is a not-for-profit organisation known as a community interest company limited by guarantee.

This means 100% of profits are invested into the community.

The enterprise was created out of a need for a support provider that supports learning disabled people to reach their personal and professional aspirations, whatever they may be, through the use of person-centred-planning.

At current we provide services to learning disabled people in the following areas:

  • Royal Borough of Kensington and Chelsea
  • London Borough of Hounslow
  • London Borough of Hillingdon
  • Other regions including, Surrey and other parts of Greater London.

CTRC CIC is registered on a number of care directories these include the west London alliance and NHS choices websites.
