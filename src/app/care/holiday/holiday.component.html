Supported Holidays

Personal holidays and trips

If you have a trip you wish to organise or want us to organise with full support, we are happy to accommodate. The cost of such a service is dependent on a number of variables such as how long you wish to travel, care services required and destination. Therefore we recommend contacting us for further information.

Activictus

CTRC operates its own holiday scheme for learning disabled adults in a number of holiday resorts abroad.

The holiday scheme is great for group activities to promote fitness, recreational activities and social development.

If you are interested in finding out upcoming dates and more about the scheme please take a look at the Activictus Website.
